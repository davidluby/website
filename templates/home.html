Motorized Bicycle

The best aspect of this project was that it did not end until I sold the bike. It turns out that adding a four-stroke engine to the frame of a cheap bicycle tends to create more problems than it solves, requiring constant maintenance in response to vibrations never meant for a pedal bike. Experiential improvement by iteration helped unveil complex systems as large sums of simple solutions.
